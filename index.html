The Best Restaurants in Hoi An: A Culinary Journey Through Lantern-Lit Streets

Curated by Linh Nguyen

The Essence

"Hoi An is not just a city; it is a kitchen where centuries of spice trade and coastal bounty have simmered into a unique culinary dialect."

In the heart of Vietnam's central coast lies a city that glows like a dying ember. Hoi An, once a bustling trading port, has preserved its spirit in the form of golden-hued alleyways and a food scene that rivals the world’s greatest capitals. This journey takes us beyond the tourist traps, into the quiet courtyards and riverside terraces where the true taste of the city resides.

High-End Authentic

Morning Glory Signature

A pioneer in preserving ancestral recipes, Morning Glory offers an elevated theatrical dining experience where the sounds of the wok and the scent of anise create an unforgettable atmosphere.

Riverside

Mango Mango

A vibrant fusion of local ingredients and global techniques, overlooking the Thu Bon River. Known for its eclectic cocktails and the best view of the Night Market lights.
